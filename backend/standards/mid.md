# MID LANE - Challenger Standards

## CS & Gold
- **CS Benchmark**: 8.5+ CS/min. Mid has the shortest wave clear path — no excuse for low CS.
- Never sacrifice a full wave for a low-probability roam.
- Recall when wave crashes into enemy tower, not when it's in the middle.

## Wave Management
- **Freeze**: Maintain 3-6 minions on your side to deny enemy CS and set up ganks.
- **Slow Push**: Stack 2-3 waves before roaming. Ensures enemy loses CS to tower while you roam.
- **Fast Push**: Clear instantly before roaming. Never leave a wave to rot under your tower.
- **Priority**: Having wave priority (your wave pushes first) means you can roam, contest scuttle, or help jungler first.

## Trading Patterns
- **Mages**: Poke enemy below 60% HP before committing to all-in. Use abilities on enemy WHILE they last-hit.
- **Assassins**: Wait for enemy to use their key defensive ability (Zhonya's, barrier, mobility spell) before committing.
- **Level 2/3 spike**: Many mid laners have kill pressure at level 2-3 (Fizz, Syndra, Zed). Respect or abuse this.
- **Cooldown trading**: Trade when enemy's key ability is on cooldown. Track it mentally (e.g., Syndra E = 16s CD early).

## Roaming
- **Rule**: Only roam AFTER crashing wave into enemy tower. Never roam with wave in middle.
- **Roam timing**: After pushing wave, you have ~20 seconds before next wave arrives. Use this window.
- **Commit**: A roam must result in a kill, Flash burn, or objective. A roam that achieves nothing = tempo loss.
- **Ping before roaming**: Ping your laner "missing" and ping the lane you're roaming to.
- **Counter-roam**: If enemy mid roams and you can't follow, hard push wave into their tower. They lose 1-2 waves of CS.
- **Rift Herald**: Be present for Herald fights at 14:00. Herald placed mid creates the most map pressure.

## Vision Control
- Ward BOTH river brushes (top and bot side). Mid has the most map impact — vision is critical.
- If you have priority, help jungler ward enemy raptors or blue buff.
- Sweeper before roaming to avoid being spotted.

## Back Timing
- Recall on cannon wave crash. Walk back to lane with full HP/mana for next trade window.
- If you get a kill, push wave into tower THEN recall. Do not recall immediately.
- Never recall with wave pushing toward you — it freezes for enemy.

## Teamfight Positioning
- **Mages/APC**: Stay at max cast range. Step inside threat range ONLY with Zhonya's available.
- **Assassins**: Wait for enemy frontline to use key CC, then flank backline. Never engage first.
- **Combo execution**: Land your full combo on priority target (enemy ADC/mid), then reposition. Do not stand still after casting.
- After winning a teamfight, immediately rotate to nearest objective.

## Matchup Awareness
- **Into assassins**: Take defensive summoner (Barrier/Exhaust). Rush Seeker's or Verdant Barrier.
- **Into mages**: Trade HP for HP early. Sustain advantages matter (Corrupting Pot, Doran's Ring).
- **Into roamers** (TF, Aurelion Sol): Hard push wave so they lose CS if they roam. Ping missing immediately.

## General
- Every death has a reason: positioning, cooldown tracking, vision failure, or macro error.
- Deaths within 30 seconds of an objective spawn are critical errors.
- Never be below 40% HP or 30% mana in contested areas without a clear escape plan.
- Track enemy jungler. If jungler is top side, play aggressive. If unknown, respect.

## Power Spike Timings
- **Lost Chapter (1300g)**: Mages spike hard. Should be first back at 5:00-6:00.
- **Dirk (1100g)**: Assassins spike hard. First back = kill pressure.
- **Level 6**: Most mid laners have kill pressure at 6. If you hit 6 first, look for all-in.
- **Level 9**: Rank 5 main ability. Mages clear waves instantly. Massive spike.
- **2-item spike**: ~20-22 min. Mages can one-shot squishies. Assassins can one-shot anyone without armor.

## Game Clock Transitions
- **0-14 min**: Laning + roaming. Push wave → roam or push wave → recall.
- **14-20 min**: Skirmishing. Contest objectives. Use priority to help jungler.
- **20+ min**: Teamfighting. Group with team. Catch side waves between fights but never miss a fight.
- **Scuttle fights (3:15)**: If you have priority, you MUST rotate. It's not optional.

## Cooldown Tracking
- Flash = 300s. If enemy mid flashed at 4:00, it's back at 9:00. All-in window.
- Barrier = 180s. Track it — if enemy used Barrier, they're killable for 3 minutes.
- Zhonya's = 120s. After enemy uses Zhonya's, they're vulnerable for 2 minutes.
- Enemy jungler Flash: if jungler flashed during a gank, they can't gank effectively for 5 minutes.

## Win Condition Awareness
- **You are the carry**: Farm safely, scale, carry teamfights. Don't coinflip roams.
- **You are the enabler**: Roam, set up plays, sacrifice CS for map impact.
- **You are behind**: Push wave, don't fight, wait for item spike. One death when behind = game over.

## Playing From Behind
- If 0/2 in lane: push wave safely with abilities, never walk up for CS in auto range.
- Farm raptors/wolves between waves if safe. Catch up in gold without risking death.
- Look for TP plays or roams where you can get assists without 1v1ing.
