# MID LANE - Challenger Standards

## CS & Gold
- **CS Benchmark**: 8.5+ CS/min. Mid lane has the shortest wave clear path - there is no excuse for low CS.
- Never sacrifice a wave for a low-value kill or roam.
- Recall when the wave is crashing into the enemy tower, not when it is in the middle of the lane.

## Wave Management
- **Freeze**: Maintain 3-6 minions on your side to deny enemy CS.
- **Slow Push**: Stack waves before roaming or before major objectives.
- **Fast Push**: Clear instantly before roaming to ensure your wave doesn't rot under your tower.
- Always clear your wave before roaming. Leaving a wave to rot under your tower is a macro error.

## Roaming
- Roam only after crashing a wave into the enemy tower.
- Never roam with a slow-push wave - you will lose CS and pressure.
- When roaming, commit to the kill. A failed roam that burns enemy Flash is acceptable. A roam that achieves nothing is a tempo loss.
- Be present for Rift Herald fights at 14:00. Herald placed mid creates the most map pressure.

## Vision Control
- Ward both river brushes (top and bot side) at all times.
- Mid lane has the most map impact - vision is critical for roam timing and tracking the enemy jungler.

## Trading Patterns
- **Mages**: Poke the enemy below 60% HP before committing to an all-in.
- **Assassins**: Wait for the enemy to use their mobility spell before engaging.
- Recognize all-in windows when the enemy has used key abilities (gap closers, shields, heals).

## Teamfight Positioning
- **Mages/APC**: Stay at maximum cast range. Step inside threat range only with peel available.
- **Assassins**: Wait for the enemy frontline to commit before diving the backline.
- After winning a teamfight, immediately rotate to the nearest objective.

## General
- Every death has a reason: positioning, cooldown tracking, vision failure, or macro error.
- Deaths within 30 seconds of an objective spawn are critical errors.
- Never be below 40% HP or 30% mana in contested areas without a clear escape plan.
