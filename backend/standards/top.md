# TOP LANE - Challenger Standards

## CS & Gold
- **CS Benchmark**: 8.0+ CS/min by 10 minutes. Below 7.0 CS/min is unacceptable.
- After winning a fight, immediately convert into tower plates or Rift Herald. Never stand idle after a kill.

## Wave Management
- **Freeze**: Maintain 3-6 minions on your side to deny enemy CS near your tower.
- **Slow Push**: Stack 3 waves before Baron/Dragon/Herald to create pressure.
- **Fast Push**: Clear instantly to roam, recall safely, or set up a dive.
- **Crash and Roam**: After crashing into enemy tower, immediately roam or take Herald.

## Teleport Usage
- Never TP back to lane unless the wave is crashing into the enemy tower.
- Use TP to impact bot lane fights, secure Dragon, or flank in teamfights.
- **Teleport Flanks**: TP behind the enemy team to cut off escapes - not to join the front line.
- A wasted TP (returning to empty lane) is a critical macro error.

## Split Push
- When ahead, split push the opposite side lane. Force the enemy to send 2 to respond.
- Rotate when your team engages - do not keep split pushing during a teamfight.
- Only duel during your power spike windows (first item, level 6, level 9).

## Vision Control
- Place deep wards in enemy jungle at 5:00 and 10:00.
- Ward the river brush before roaming after a wave crash.

## Objective Priority
- Pre-20min: After winning a top fight, take Rift Herald immediately.
- Post-20min: Push tower or rotate to Baron setup after winning a fight.

## Teamfight Positioning
- Frontline/Tank: Stay between the enemy and your backline. Body-block skillshots for carries.
- Split-pusher: Apply side pressure, then TP flank when your team engages.

## General
- Every death has a reason: positioning, cooldown tracking, vision failure, or macro error.
- Deaths within 30 seconds of an objective spawn are critical errors.
- Never be below 40% HP or 30% mana in contested areas without a clear escape plan.
