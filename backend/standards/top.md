# TOP LANE - Challenger Standards

## CS & Gold
- **CS Benchmark**: 8.0+ CS/min. Below 7.0 is unacceptable.
- After a kill, immediately crash wave into tower → take plates or Herald. Never stand idle after a kill.
- Know your first item spike. Play aggressively after completing it (Stridebreaker, Trinity, BORK).

## Wave Management
- **Freeze**: Maintain 3-6 enemy minions on your side. Zone enemy off CS. Only freeze when ahead or when jungler is nearby.
- **Slow Push**: Stack 3 waves before crashing. Use before roaming, recalling, or objective fights.
- **Fast Push**: Clear instantly to roam, recall, or set up a dive with jungler.
- **Crash and Roam**: After crashing cannon wave into tower, roam mid or take Herald. Never stay and watch tower kill minions.
- **Bounce back**: After crashing, the wave bounces back to you. Use this to set up a freeze or safe recall.

## Trading & Laning
- **Trading stance**: Stand inside the enemy minion wave when they want to last-hit. They must choose: CS or trade back.
- **Minion aggro**: Auto-attacking enemy champion draws minion aggro. Early game, 6 minions do more damage than a champion. Trade, then step into bush to drop aggro.
- **Level 1-2 cheese**: Many top laners (Darius, Renekton, Riven) have kill pressure at level 1-2. If you have this advantage, use it. If enemy does, respect it and give CS.
- **All-in windows**: Level 6 spike, first item completion, enemy used key cooldown (Darius E, Fiora W, Jax E). Track these.
- **Short vs long trades**: Know your champion's trade pattern. Renekton = short (E in, W, Q, E out). Darius = long (stack passive to 5). Never take a long trade on a short-trade champion.

## Teleport Usage
- Never TP back to lane unless wave is crashing into enemy tower (you lose nothing).
- Use TP to impact bot fights, secure Dragon, or flank teamfights.
- **TP flanks**: TP behind enemy team to cut off escapes. Never TP to frontline.
- Wasted TP (returning to empty lane or TP cancelled) is a critical macro error.
- After 14min, hold TP for teamfight impact. Do not use it to catch a wave.

## Split Push
- Only split when you can 1v1 or 1v2 the enemy response.
- Split the OPPOSITE side of the next objective. If Baron is up, split bot.
- Watch minimap every 3 seconds while splitting. If 3+ enemies are missing, back off.
- Join teamfights via TP flank when your team engages. Do not keep splitting during a 4v5.

## Back Timing
- Recall after crashing a cannon wave (every 3rd wave). This gives you ~30 seconds before wave returns.
- Never recall with wave in middle of lane — it freezes for enemy.
- If you get a kill, crash wave THEN recall. Do not recall immediately.

## Teamfight Positioning
- **Tanks/Engagers** (Ornn, Malphite, Sion): Frontline between enemy and your backline. Engage when team is grouped.
- **Bruisers/Divers** (Camille, Irelia, Jax): Flank or wait for enemy frontline to commit, then dive backline.
- **Split-pushers** (Fiora, Tryndamere): Apply side pressure, TP flank when team engages.
- After winning a teamfight, immediately rotate to nearest objective. Do not chase.

## General
- Every death has a reason: bad trade timing, no vision of jungler, or disrespecting enemy power spike.
- Deaths within 30 seconds of an objective spawn are critical errors.
- Track enemy jungler. If jungler is bot side, play aggressive. If unknown, play safe.
- Never be below 40% HP in lane without vision of enemy jungler.

## Power Spike Timings
- **Level 1-2**: Darius, Renekton, Riven have kill pressure. Respect or abuse.
- **Level 6**: Most top laners spike hard (Darius R execute, Fiora R heal, Camille R lockdown).
- **First item** (~12-14 min): Trinity/Stridebreaker/BORK completion = massive 1v1 spike.
- **Tabis/Steelcaps rush**: Against heavy AD, this one item wins trades you previously lost.
- **Level 9**: Rank 5 main ability. Many top laners clear waves instantly here.

## Matchup Adjustments
- **Into ranged top** (Vayne, Quinn, Kennen): Concede CS levels 1-5. All-in at 6 with full HP + ignite.
- **Into scaling** (Nasus, Kayle): Punish hard levels 1-8. Freeze and zone. Do NOT let them free farm.
- **Into lane bullies** (Darius, Renekton): Short trades only. Never extended fight. Give CS if needed.
- **Ignite vs TP**: If enemy has ignite, they win all-ins until you have item advantage. Respect it.
- **Doran's Shield**: Take into poke/ranged matchups. Doran's Blade into melee matchups you can trade in.

## Game Clock Transitions
- **0-14 min**: Laning. Win lane or survive. Take Herald if you have prio.
- **14-20 min**: Split or group based on comp. If you're a split-pusher, split. If you're a tank, group.
- **20+ min**: Play around Baron. Split opposite side OR group for teamfights.
- After first tower falls, the laning phase is OVER. Transition immediately.

## Cooldown Tracking
- Flash = 300s. If enemy top flashed at 5:00, all-in them at 6:00-7:00 (no escape).
- TP = 360s. Track enemy TP. If they TP'd bot, you have 6 minutes of pressure top.
- Ignite = 180s. After enemy uses ignite, you have 3 minutes where you win all-ins.

## Win Condition Awareness
- **You are the split-pusher**: Never group for teamfights. Apply side pressure and TP flank.
- **You are the tank/engager**: Group with team. Your job is engage, not side lane CS.
- **You are behind**: Freeze under tower, farm safely, TP for teamfights. Do NOT try to 1v1.

## Playing From Behind
- If 0/2: freeze wave near your tower. Let jungler come for a gank. Never fight alone.
- Build defensive (Tabis, Bramble Vest) to survive lane, not offensive to "come back."
- Your value when behind: TP flanks, teamfight utility, peeling for carries. Not 1v1 dueling.
- Give up plates. Plates are not worth dying for. Ever.
