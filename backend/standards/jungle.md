# JUNGLE - Challenger Standards

## Clear Speed
- **Full clear (6 camps)** must be completed by 3:15-3:30. Slower clears indicate pathing or mechanics issues.
- Always have a clear plan for your next 3 camps before recalling. Aimless pathing is a tempo loss.
- Track enemy jungle camps. If you see the enemy jungler bot side, immediately take their top side camps.

## Gank Timing
- First gank should happen between 2:30-3:30 depending on lane states.
- Never gank a pushed-in lane - it is a wasted gank.
- After a successful gank, invade the enemy jungler's next camp to deny gold and XP.

## Objective Control
- **Dragon**: Spawns at 5:00 - be in position by 4:30. Place a ward in Dragon pit at 4:00. Coordinate with bot lane.
- **Baron**: Spawns at 20:00 - set up vision at 19:00.
- **Scuttle Crab**: Secure after your first clear to gain vision and movement speed buff.
- Never use Smite on non-objective camps when Dragon or Baron is alive and contestable.
- Missing Smite on Baron or Dragon is a critical error.

## Pathing
- Always path toward the side of the map where you can create the most pressure.
- After winning a fight, immediately move to the nearest high-value objective.
- Leash communication: Starting without leash on certain champions (Amumu, Sejuani) costs 10-15 seconds.

## Vision
- Place deep wards in enemy jungle after successful ganks.
- Track enemy jungler position based on camp timers after their first clear.

## Teamfight Positioning
- Engage only when your team is in position. Engaging when your team is split is a critical error.
- After winning a teamfight, immediately move to Baron or Dragon.
- Use Rift Herald to destroy towers, not as a teamfight tool unless the fight is already won.

## General
- Every death has a reason: positioning, cooldown tracking, vision failure, or macro error.
- Deaths within 30 seconds of an objective spawn are critical errors.
- Never be below 40% HP or 30% mana near objectives without a clear escape plan.
