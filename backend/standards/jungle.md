# JUNGLE - Challenger Standards

## Clear Speed & Pathing
- **Full clear (6 camps)**: Must complete by 3:15-3:30. Slower = pathing or kiting issues.
- **3-camp into gank**: Red → Raptors → Gromp (or Blue → Gromp → Red) for level 3 gank at 2:30-3:00.
- Always have a plan for next 3 camps before recalling. Aimless pathing = tempo loss.
- Path toward the side where you can create pressure (weak lane, no Flash, pushed enemy).
- After successful gank, take the nearest enemy camp to deny gold/XP.

## Tracking Enemy Jungle
- **CS count method**: At 3:30, check enemy jungler's CS. 4 CS = 4 camps cleared (they skipped 2). Tells you which side they started.
- If enemy jungler shows bot, immediately take their top camps (Gromp, Blue, Wolves).
- Track respawn timers: camps respawn at 2:15 after cleared. If you saw them take Raptors at 5:00, it respawns at 7:15.
- If enemy jungler is dead, invade immediately. Take 2+ camps before they respawn.

## Gank Timing
- **First gank window**: 2:30-3:30 depending on clear path.
- Never gank a pushed-in lane — the enemy is under their tower.
- Best gank timing: when your laner has a freeze or the enemy is pushed past river.
- **Counter-gank setup**: If you know enemy jungler is pathing to a lane, be there first. Counter-ganks are higher value than ganks.
- After a successful gank: push wave with laner → take plates or objective. Never leave immediately.

## Objective Control
- **Dragon**: Spawns at 5:00. Be in position by 4:30. Ward pit at 4:00.
- **Rift Herald**: Spawns at 14:00. Take it after winning a top/mid fight.
- **Baron**: Spawns at 20:00. Set up vision at 19:00. Never start Baron without knowing enemy jungler's position.
- **Smite management**: Never use Smite on camps when Dragon/Baron is alive and contestable within 60 seconds.
- Missing Smite on Baron/Dragon is a critical error. Practice Smite timing: 900 damage at level 1, scales with level.

## Tempo & Decision Making
- **Farming vs ganking**: If all lanes are pushed, farm. If a lane is gankable, gank. Never sit in a bush waiting for 30+ seconds.
- **Reset timing**: Recall when you have enough gold for a component AND no objective is spawning within 60 seconds.
- **After teamfight win**: Immediately move to Baron/Dragon. Do not recall, do not farm camps.
- **Invading**: Only invade when you have lane priority on the adjacent lane. Invading without priority = death.

## Teamfight Role
- **Engage junglers** (Amumu, Sejuani, Zac): Wait for team to be in range. Flash+engage only when 3+ teammates can follow.
- **Assassin junglers** (Kha'Zix, Evelynn): Wait for enemy to use key cooldowns, then flank backline.
- **Bruiser junglers** (Viego, Lee Sin): Peel for carry OR dive backline depending on team comp. Decide before fight starts.
- After winning a fight, secure objective before chasing kills.

## General
- Every death has a reason: bad pathing, face-checking without vision, or fighting without Smite.
- Deaths within 30 seconds of an objective spawn are critical errors.
- Never be below 40% HP near objectives without Smite available.
- If you die with Smite up near Dragon/Baron, that's a mechanical failure.

## Power Spike Timings
- **Level 3 (3 camps)**: First gank window. Most junglers have kill pressure here.
- **Level 6**: Massive spike for ult-dependent junglers (Amumu, Nocturne, Vi). Gank immediately.
- **First item**: ~10-12 min. Your clear speed and duel potential spike hard.
- **Smite upgrade**: Unlocks at 5 Smite uses. Prioritize using Smite on cooldown early.

## Game Clock Transitions
- **0-5 min**: First clear + first gank/invade. Set the tempo.
- **5-14 min**: Objective focus. Dragon, Rift Herald, ganks around objectives.
- **14-20 min**: Voidgrubs/Herald done. Transition to teamfight setup. Group for Dragons.
- **20+ min**: Baron focus. Never be on opposite side of map from Baron after 20 min.

## Cooldown Tracking
- Flash = 300s. If enemy laner flashed at 3:00, gank them again at 5:00 (they have no escape).
- Smite CD = 90s. Never use Smite on camps within 60s of objective spawn.
- Track enemy jungler's Smite. If they used it on a camp, contest the next objective.

## Win Condition Awareness
- **Your team has a hypercarry**: Play around them. Gank their lane, give them kills, peel in fights.
- **Your team has early game comp**: Force fights and objectives before 25 min. You outscale nothing.
- **You are the carry** (Yi, Bel'Veth): Power farm. Only gank if it's free. Scale to 2 items.
- **You are behind**: Farm your own jungle, avoid enemy jungler, look for counter-ganks only.

## Playing From Behind
- If 0/3: stop forcing plays. Farm camps, take safe ganks only (enemy overextended with no Flash).
- Give up contested objectives if team isn't in position. Dying for a lost Dragon = 2 losses.
- Look for shutdown bounties on overextended enemies.
- Catch-up XP: take every camp on cooldown. Jungle XP catches you up faster than lane.
