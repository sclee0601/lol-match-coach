# BOT LANE (ADC) - Challenger Standards

## CS & Gold
- **CS Benchmark**: 8.0+ CS/min. ADC is the most CS-dependent role. Missing CS under tower is unacceptable.
- Know your first item power spike (Kraken Slayer, Galeforce, Infinity Edge). Play aggressively after completing it.
- Recall when the wave is crashing into the enemy tower. Coordinate with support to ensure the wave is in a good state.

## Laning Phase
- Stay behind your support at all times. Never step forward to CS if the enemy has an engage ability available.
- Use attack-move to kite in trades. Standing still while auto-attacking is a mechanical error.
- Coordinate with your support on whether they should peel for you or engage. Misaligned expectations cause deaths.

## Wave Management
- **Freeze**: Let your support zone the enemy while you freeze near your tower to deny enemy CS.
- **Slow Push**: Stack waves before Dragon fights to create pressure.
- **Fast Push**: Clear instantly before recalling or rotating to objectives.

## Vision Control
- Coordinate with support to ensure river wards are placed at 1:30.
- After recalling, always check if river vision is up before walking back to lane.

## Teamfight Positioning
- Stay at maximum attack range at all times. Any step inside the enemy threat range without peel is a positioning error.
- Identify the highest-threat enemy and maintain maximum distance from them.
- Your job is to deal sustained damage from a safe position. Do not chase kills into dangerous positions.
- Be present for every Dragon fight. ADC damage is critical for securing Dragon and Baron.

## Late Game
- In late game teamfights, deal sustained damage from a safe position.
- Do not overextend to chase kills - your death is worth more than any kill you can get.
- Peel awareness: communicate with your support about whether they should peel or engage.

## General
- Every death has a reason: positioning, cooldown tracking, vision failure, or macro error.
- Deaths within 30 seconds of an objective spawn are critical errors.
- Never be below 40% HP in a contested area without a clear escape plan.
