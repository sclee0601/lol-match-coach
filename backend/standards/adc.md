# BOT LANE (ADC) - Challenger Standards

## CS & Gold
- **CS Benchmark**: 8.0+ CS/min. ADC is the most CS-dependent role. Missing CS under tower is unacceptable.
- **Under-tower CS**: Melee minions = tower shot + tower shot + auto. Caster minions = auto + tower shot + auto (or support helps).
- Know your first item spike. Play aggressively after completing it.
- Recall when cannon wave crashes into enemy tower. Coordinate with support.

## Laning Phase
- **Level 2 timing**: First wave + 3 melee of second wave. If you hit 2 first, step forward aggressively.
- **Positioning**: Stand behind your support at all times. Never step forward to CS if enemy has engage available.
- **Trading**: Only trade when enemy ADC goes for a last-hit (they can't auto you back). Auto → step back.
- **Attack-move kiting**: Use attack-move to kite in trades. Standing still while auto-attacking is a mechanical error.
- **Spacing**: Maintain max auto range from enemy support. If Nautilus/Leona can hook you, you're too close.

## Wave Management
- **Freeze**: Let support zone while you freeze near tower. Denies enemy CS and sets up ganks.
- **Slow Push**: Stack waves before Dragon fights to create pressure.
- **Fast Push**: Clear instantly before recalling or rotating to objectives.
- **Never push without vision**: If river is unwarded and you're pushed past middle, you're gankable.

## Recall Timing
- Recall on cannon wave crash (every 3rd wave). This gives max time before wave returns.
- Recall WITH support. Never recall alone leaving support in a 1v2.
- If below 40% HP and no sustain, recall immediately. Dying costs more than missing 1 wave.

## Teamfight Positioning
- **Max range**: Stay at maximum auto-attack range at ALL times. Any step inside enemy threat range without peel = positioning error.
- **Target priority**: Hit the closest enemy you can safely auto. Do NOT walk past frontline to hit backline.
- **Kiting direction**: Kite backwards (away from enemy). Never kite forward into the enemy team.
- **Flash/Dash usage**: Save E/Flash for enemy dive. Never use escape abilities to chase.
- **Identify the threat**: Before fight starts, identify who will dive you (Zed, Camille, Vi). Position to make their engage as long as possible.

## Objective Fights
- Be present for EVERY Dragon fight. ADC damage is critical for securing Dragon/Baron.
- Position behind pit wall during Baron/Dragon. Never stand inside pit where you can be engaged on.
- If team is doing Baron, watch the flank — your job is DPS on Baron AND watching for enemy engage.

## Late Game
- Never be alone in a side lane without vision. One death = lost Baron/game.
- Group with team after 25 minutes. Split pushing as ADC is almost always wrong.
- In late game, your death is worth more than any kill. Play safe, deal sustained damage.

## General
- Every death has a reason: positioning, cooldown tracking, vision failure, or macro error.
- Deaths within 30 seconds of an objective spawn are critical errors.
- Never be below 40% HP in a contested area without a clear escape plan.
- Track enemy assassin cooldowns. If Zed used R 20 seconds ago, you have 80 seconds of safety.

## Power Spike Timings
- **BF Sword (1300g)**: Should be first back at 5:00-5:30. If you have it and enemy doesn't, force trades.
- **First item completion**: ~12-14 min. This is your biggest spike — play aggressive.
- **2-item spike**: ~22-24 min. You start carrying teamfights here.
- **Level 6**: Some ADCs spike hard (Kai'Sa, Lucian, MF). Look for all-in.
- If you're down a full item, do NOT fight. Farm until you catch up.

## Matchup Adjustments
- **Into engage support** (Nautilus, Leona): Stand behind minion wave. Give 1-2 CS rather than eat a hook.
- **Into poke support** (Zyra, Brand): Sustain through with Doran's Blade + pots. All-in when they waste cooldowns.
- **Short-range vs long-range**: If you're Kai'Sa into Caitlyn, concede early CS. You outscale at 2 items.
- **With engage support**: Follow their engage immediately. Hesitation = wasted cooldown.
- **With enchanter support**: Play for scaling. Trade when shield/heal is available.

## Game Clock Transitions
- **0-14 min**: Laning. Focus on CS, trading, and surviving ganks.
- **14-20 min**: Skirmishing. Group for objectives but catch side waves between fights.
- **20+ min**: Teamfighting. Never be alone. Your death = lost Baron.
- After first tower falls, rotate mid. Do not stay bot farming alone.

## Cooldown Tracking
- Flash = 300s (5 min). If enemy ADC flashed at 3:00, it's back at 8:00.
- Heal = 240s (4 min). Track enemy bot lane summoners after every trade.
- If enemy support has no Flash, ping it and look for engage.
